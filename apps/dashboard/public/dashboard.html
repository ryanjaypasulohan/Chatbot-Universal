How It Works

A quick 4-step guide to get your chatbot live on your website.

  1. Step 1: Add your website domain details in the "Add chatbot to your website" form.
  2. Step 2: Customize your virtual assistant’s settings and appearance in the Widget tab.
  3. Step 3: Copy the generated script snippet from the Embed script card.
  4. Step 4: Paste the script into your website's header/footer (or HTML) to go live.
